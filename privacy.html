Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective for NEET 2026 Phod Denge app (com.phoddenge.neet)

1 Who We Are

Phod Denge ("we", "us", "our") operates the NEET 2026 Phod Denge mobile application (package: com.phoddenge.neet) and the website at phoddenge.com. We are an independent ed-tech product based in Mumbai, India. Contact: phoddenge.team@gmail.com

2 Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Google Account (Sign-In): Name, email address, and profile photo provided via Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication). Used solely for account identification.
  • Study Activity: Questions attempted, scores, streaks, and test history — stored locally on your device using SQLite and optionally synced to Firebase Firestore.
  • Mood Check-in: Daily mood entries from the MindSpace section, stored only on your device (local storage). Not transmitted to our servers.
  • Payment Data: Subscription payments (₹249/month) are processed by Razorpay. We do not store card or bank details on our servers.
  • Device Information: Basic device metadata (OS version, app version) for crash reporting and compatibility purposes.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and provide personalised study progress
  • To deliver and improve the app experience
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To send important service-related notifications (not marketing spam)
  • To analyse aggregate (anonymised) usage patterns for product improvement

4 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure (Firestore, asia-south1 region — Mumbai). Firebase is ISO 27001 certified and SOC 2/3 compliant. Study data and question databases are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

5 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is shared only with:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication and database services
  • Razorpay — for payment processing only
  • Legal authorities — only if required by applicable law

6 Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students preparing for NEET, primarily aged 16–18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the app

To exercise any of these rights, email us at phoddenge.team@gmail.com

8 Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9 Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to external resources (e.g. NCERT, NTA). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. Please review their privacy policies independently.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via in-app notification or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
